Alexander Lewis

The Work

A multi-instrumentalist/producer in his 20's from Vacaville (cow town) California. Has been poducing music for more than two years. He was introduced to producing by friends Ivan (from Brass tracks) and Jnthn Stein. At the time he was HEAVILY influenced by Bobby Tank. Then he got heavy into SBTRKT, James Blake, Ryan Hemsworth. What ultimately got him into making beats like how he is today was Team Supreme. Those weekly beat cyphers were a God send.
