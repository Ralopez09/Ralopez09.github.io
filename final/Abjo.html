The Work

The Nature of Things is an EP / short beat tape / beat suite that is meant for the lonely stoner, the beat enthusiast, the party whore, the beatmaker and the producer, the average audiophile and digital music lover all the same. It is celestial, dream-like, synthetic and cybernetic hip hop type music, meant to explain the nature of things as never explained before: through the stars, and all we can see from our decreasingly green earth.
